Maria Cruz

Chief Executive Officer at Space City Tech

Maria Cruz is a first-generation high school student at Lamar High School who founded Space City Tech out of her passion for coding and desire to give back to her community. Being a first-generation student, Maria understands the importance of access to resources and opportunities, especially in the technology industry. She recognizes that many underrepresented students, particularly those from low-income backgrounds, lack access to these opportunities and resources, which can limit their potential for success. She believes that by providing access to resources and opportunities for underrepresented students, they can also become change-makers in their communities.
